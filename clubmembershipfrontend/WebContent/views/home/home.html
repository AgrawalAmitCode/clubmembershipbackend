What We Do

Situated in Pune, the People's Sports Club provides a combination of natural beauty with a warm and friendly atmosphere for you and your family.


The club's large recreational facility offers something for everyone. Rent a basketball court with your friends for hours of fun and play. Experience our state of the art indoor hockey rink, or challenge your skills at the People's Driving Range and Mini Golf Center. Expand your child's summer fun at our safe and well supervised Day Camp.


With the increase in materialism and the need for excellence, the people around the world now look forward for world class products, services, facilities and hospitality. Rapidly changing life styles, particularly in the Metros, have increased the consciousness about health, mental peace and spirituality amongst the urban population. People now look forward and spend time for achieving the aforesaid objectives and engage themselves in sports and leisure activities more than ever.


At Peoples Sports Club, an effort has been made to provide world class facilities and infrastructure for the promotion of sports and health.



        Indoor Fun

Indoor facilities are where we excel. We offer indoor games facilities like TableTennis, Billiards, Health Club and Squash

        Outdoor Fun

Outdoor games have always been our forte. Our outdoor facilities include swimming,lawn tennis,cricket and many more

      Leisure Activities

Leisure facilities provide you great places to hold meetings and parties. These include Restaurents and Banquet halls
